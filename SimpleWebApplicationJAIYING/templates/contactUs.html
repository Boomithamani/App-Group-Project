Account Management


FAQ
How do I create an account?
•   To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the website and fill in the required details, including your email address and password.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
•  Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and enter your email address. A password reset link will be sent to your inbox.
How can I delete my account?
•  If you wish to delete your account, go to settings and scroll down to see "Delete Account".
How can I update my email address?
•  To update your email address, log into your account, go to "Account Settings," and edit your contact information.
Can I change my username?
•  Go to "Account Settings" and click the pencil icon next to your username to make changes.

Order & Delivery


FAQ
How do I check the delivery status of my order?
•  An email will be sent to you once the order is dispatched and you may check your order status there.
•  Alternatively, we partner with SPX Express so you can key in your order reference number on their website.
What do the order status mean?
•  Order Received: Your order has been received and is being processed.
•  Order Dispatched: Your order has been dispatched and is on its way to you.
•  Order Delivered: Your order has been delivered to you.
•  Order Cancelled: Your order has been cancelled.
What should I do when the tracking link isn't working?
•  If the tracking link isn't working, please try again after a few minutes or contact customer service for assistance.
What are the shipping fees?
•  The shipping fees depend on the delivery location and order weight. You can check the exact fees during checkout before finalizing your order.
Do you offer free shipping?
•  Yes, we offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount. Please check the promotions page for more details on eligibility.

Return & Exchange


FAQ
What is your return policy?
•  Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. They must be unused and in original packaging. Please visit the Returns & Exchanges page for further instructions.
How do I exchange an item?
•  To exchange an item, please contact our customer service team with your order details. They will guide you through the exchange process.
How long does it take to process a return?
•  Once we receive the returned item, please allow up to 7 business days for the return to be processed and for your refund to be issued.
How will I be refunded for my return?
•  Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at checkout. Please note that it may take a few business days for the refund to appear in your account.
Do I have to pay for return shipping?
•  Return shipping is typically at the customer's expense, unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part. Please check our Returns & Exchanges page for further details.
What if my item arrived damaged or defective?
•  If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will arrange for a return and issue a full refund or send a replacement.
Can I return or exchange items purchased with a gift card?
•  Yes, items purchased with a gift card can be returned or exchanged as long as they meet the return policy criteria. Refunds will be credited to the original gift card.

Payment


FAQ
What payment methods do you accept?
•  We accept major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options available at checkout.
Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?
•  Currently, we only allow one payment method per order. Please choose the payment method that works best for you during checkout.
How do I apply a promo code to my order?
•  At checkout, you will find an option to enter your promo code. Simply input the code, and the discount will be applied to your total order amount.
Is it safe to enter my credit card details on your website?
•  Yes, our website uses SSL encryption to securely process your payment information. Your data is protected throughout the payment process.
Can I pay with a gift card?
•  Yes, we accept gift cards as a payment method. You can use them just like credit or debit cards at checkout.
What should I do if my payment is declined?
•  If your payment is declined, please check your card details and ensure there are sufficient funds in your account. If the issue persists, contact your bank or try another payment method.
